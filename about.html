👁️ Welcome to The Beholder's Gazette

Founded in 1492 DR, The Beholder's Gazette is the realm's premier source for satirical news, DM tools, and everything in between. We see all truths — especially the ones we made up.

Our mission is simple: provide Dungeon Masters with high-quality, free tools while keeping everyone entertained. Because let's face it, if the DM isn't having fun, nobody is.

🛠️ Our Tools

Every tool on this site is designed with one goal: make your D&D sessions better. Here's what we offer:

  • Dynamic Economy Tracker: 200+ D&D items with prices that fluctuate every 15 minutes. Perfect for adding realism to your campaign's economy.
  • NPC Generator: Create full NPCs with stats, personality, appearance, dialogue, secrets, and quest hooks. Save them to your grimoire for later.
  • Loot Generator: DMG-compliant treasure generation with coins, gems, art objects, and magic items. Export to JSON or copy to clipboard.
  • Encounter Builder: Build balanced encounters with synergy suggestions, terrain hazards, morale checks, and full initiative tracking.
  • Tavern Rumor Generator: 100+ rumors across 6 categories with truth values. Perfect for session starters or downtime activities.
  • Satirical News: D&D-themed parody articles that mock real-world politics, economics, and current events.

❓ FAQ

Is this site free?

Yes! All tools are completely free. We accept voluntary donations via Patreon to keep the servers running and the bards fed.

Can I use this for my campaign?

Absolutely! That's what it's for. Generate NPCs, loot, encounters, and rumors to your heart's content.

Is the economy tracker accurate?

It's accurate to the DMG base prices, with random fluctuations for realism. Use it as a guide, not gospel.

Can I contribute rumors or articles?

Currently, contributions are session-only. We're working on a submission system for the future!

Why is everything satire?

Because D&D is supposed to be fun. If we can make you laugh while you prepare your session, we've done our job.
